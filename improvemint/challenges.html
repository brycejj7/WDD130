Grooming at The Magic Kennel

Here at the Magic Kennel, we offer grooming for all dogs. This includes hair trimming, nail clipping, and washing. We will treat your dog as if they were our own. We have all sorts of shampoos and conditioners. From scented to unscented, oatmeal infused to help with dry skin, or even just hypoallergenic shampoo to help visitors who are allergic to animals. We have different packages for grooming. There are packages for just nail trimming or just a wash. If you want a hair trimming, it includes a wash. You can also just get a full grooming session that includes the hair trim, nail clipping, and wash.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost?

Depending on the size of your group, price can range from $200-$300. The more people in your group, the more of a discount. Call/Email for an exact estimate using our customer service contact below.

What if I fall in?

Falling in is common, and we take safety very seriously. We have safety measures in place to prevent any falls, but it is definitely part of the experience. If you do fall in, we have very experienced tour guides that are certified in all life saving programs to be able to keep your trip worry-free.

What is there to see on the trip?

Wildlife is common on all of our routes, and no matter your experience level, we will see many, many rapids.

Is lunch provided?

Yes, the menu varies by booking, but if you have any dietary restrictions we would love to accomodate. Just contact the CS line below at least a week in advance prior to your trip.
